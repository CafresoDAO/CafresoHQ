# Hantavirus 2026 Contact Tracing Updates

As of May 8, 2026, contact tracing for the Andes hantavirus outbreak on the MV Hondius cruise ship involves multiple countries and agencies, including the CDC, WHO, and UK Health Security Agency. Over 30 passengers who disembarked in Saint Helena have been traced, with hospitalizations reported in South Africa, the Netherlands, Germany, Saint Helena, Spain, France, and Switzerland. Epidemiologists are investigating transmission links, including two flights connected to an ill passenger, while a live tracker at hantavirustracker.io provides ongoing maps, news, and data.
