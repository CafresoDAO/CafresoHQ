# Initial Reports of Hantavirus 2026 Outbreak

The Hantavirus 2026 outbreak was first reported on May 2, 2026, linked to the MV Hondius cruise ship in the Atlantic Ocean, with 147 passengers and crew onboard and 34 having disembarked. Initial cases involved severe respiratory illness, confirmed as Andes virus by WHO on May 6, with reports of 8-11 cases (including 6-9 confirmed, 2 probable/suspected) and 3 deaths as of May 8-12. The ship docked in Tenerife on May 10 for disembarkation and repatriation, with ongoing monitoring by WHO, CDC, and ECDC expecting more cases.
