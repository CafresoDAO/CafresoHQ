# DAO Treasury Management Strategies

## Core Principles

**1. Asset Diversification**
- Spreading holdings across multiple asset classes reduces single-point risk
- Protects against volatility in the native token
- Improves liquidity and extends operational runway
- Typical mix: stablecoins (40-60%), ETH/BTC (20-30%), native token (20-30%)

**2. Reserve Ratios & Budget Discipline**
- Maintain minimum 12-18 months operating expenses in liquid stables
- Separate strategic reserves from operational budgets
- Cash flows in DAOs are unpredictable → conservative budgeting essential

**3. Transparency & Reporting**
- Regular treasury reports build trust and accountability
- On-chain visibility of all transactions
- Clear policies on spending thresholds and approval processes

## Governance Structure

**Treasury Management Committee**
- Most DAOs establish dedicated subcommittees for treasury oversight
- Separation of powers: proposal → approval → execution
- Multi-sig controls for large expenditures (see [[governance-voting-parameters]])

**Strategic Planning**
- Set clear financial objectives aligned with DAO mission
- Prioritize long-term sustainability over short-term gains
- Regular rebalancing based on market conditions and runway needs

## Risk Management Framework

**Key Risks to Address:**
- Smart contract risk (custody solutions)
- Market volatility (diversification + hedging)
- Operational risk (access controls, key management)
- Governance attacks (quorum requirements, time locks)

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Use battle-tested custody solutions (Gnosis Safe, etc.)
- Implement graduated spending limits with escalating approval requirements
- Stress test runway under bear market scenarios
- Diversify stablecoin exposure (USDC, DAI, USDT split)

## Allocation Best Practices

**Operational Bucket (40-50%)**
- High-liquidity stablecoins for immediate expenses
- 12-18 month runway minimum

**Strategic Reserve (30-40%)**
- Mix of blue-chip crypto assets (ETH, BTC)
- Provides upside exposure and portfolio balance

**Native Token Reserve (10-20%)**
- Used for incentives, partnerships, future fundraising
- Links to [[sns-treasury-and-initial-token-distribution]]

**Growth/Investment (0-10%)**
- DeFi yield strategies (conservative)
- Strategic investments in ecosystem projects

## Common Pitfalls

- Over-concentration in native token → death spiral risk during bear markets
- Insufficient stablecoin reserves → forced to sell native token at worst times
- Lack of spending policies → treasury drain without accountability
- No rebalancing discipline → portfolio drift into high-risk positions
