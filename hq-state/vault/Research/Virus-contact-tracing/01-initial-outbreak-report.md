# Initial Outbreak Report: MV Hondius

An outbreak of Andes virus (a type of hantavirus) was identified in April-May 2026 aboard the Dutch-flagged cruise ship MV *Hondius*.

- **Vessel**: MV *Hondius*
- **Timeline**: First cases identified in April, officially reported to WHO and CDC around May 2, 2026.
- **Population**: 147 passengers and crew from 23 different countries.
- **Initial Cases**: As of mid-May, reports indicated approximately 9 confirmed cases and 3 deaths.
- **US Connection**: At least 18 passengers have returned to the United States and are being monitored.

This event is the starting point for tracing all affected individuals. The next steps will involve trying to locate a [[passenger-and-crew-manifest]] to build out the [[country-breakdown]] and [[us-state-breakdown]].
