# Passenger Nationality & Repatriation Breakdown

This note details the distribution of passengers from the [[03-mv-hondius-passenger-crew-manifest|MV Hondius manifest]] by country, based on repatriation flight data from the unofficial EpiTrace tracker. This is the primary list for initiating contact tracing efforts globally.

## Repatriation by Country (Post-Tenerife Docking)

*   **United Kingdom:** 22 passengers
*   **Netherlands:** 26 passengers
*   **United States:** 18 passengers
*   **Spain:** 14 passengers
*   **France:** 5 passengers
*   **Canada:** 4 passengers
*   **Australia & New Zealand:** Numbers pending; evacuation flight scheduled.
*   **Turkey:** Numbers pending; government charter confirmed.

## US Passenger Distribution

The 18 US passengers were repatriated to two locations for monitoring and treatment:

*   **Nebraska:** 16 passengers to University of Nebraska Medical Center (UNMC) in Omaha.
*   **Georgia:** 2 passengers to Emory University Hospital in Atlanta.

## Critical Early Disembarkations

These individuals left the ship before the main quarantine in Tenerife and represent a high priority for tracing.

*   **St. Helena (Apr 21-24):** 32 guests disembarked, including the Dutch index couple. These individuals dispersed internationally from St. Helena.
*   **Ascension Island (Apr 27):** 2 guests (1 British, 1 American) were medically evacuated.
