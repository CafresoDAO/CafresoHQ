# MV Hondius Passenger & Crew Manifest

Based on multiple news sources and outbreak trackers, the MV Hondius departed from Ushuaia, Argentina, on April 1, 2026, carrying a total of 147 people. This manifest is a crucial starting point for contact tracing the [[02-mv-hondius-outbreak-vector|hantavirus outbreak]].

## Breakdown

*   **Total Passengers:** 88
*   **Total Crew:** 59
*   **Total Onboard:** 147

## Known Demographics

While a full passenger list has not been publicly released, some details are available:

*   **Spanish Nationals:** 14 passengers were confirmed to be from Spain. They were scheduled to be transferred to the Gómez Ulla Hospital in Madrid for quarantine.
*   **Other Nationalities:** The remaining passengers and crew were from various "respective countries," but a specific breakdown is not yet available in the public record.
