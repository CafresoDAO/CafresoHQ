# Initial Outbreak Summary: MV Hondius 2026

An outbreak of Hantavirus, specifically the Andes virus, occurred on the Dutch-flagged cruise ship MV Hondius in April-May 2026. The incident was reported to international health organizations like the WHO, CDC, and ECDC on May 2, 2026.

Key details as of mid-May 2026:
- **Location:** Atlantic Ocean
- **Vessel:** MV Hondius (Dutch-flagged)
- **Virus:** Andes virus
- **Impact:** At least 9 confirmed cases and 3 deaths.
- **Scope:** A multi-country incident involving passengers and crew from 23 nations.
