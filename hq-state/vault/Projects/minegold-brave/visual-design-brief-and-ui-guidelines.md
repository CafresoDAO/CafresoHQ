# Visual Design Brief and UI Guidelines

The project's visual and user experience direction is guided by a `DESIGN.md` file. This document serves as a high-level brief for designers, ensuring consistency across the application's interface.

According to the brief, it covers the following key areas:

*   Design direction and tone
*   Brand differentiation
*   Color palette
*   Typography
*   Elevation & depth
*   Structural zones
*   Spacing & rhythm
*   Component patterns
*   Motion and animation
*   Constraints
*   Signature details

This brief is the foundation for the application's look and feel, influencing the work done within the [[frontend-and-backend-workflows|frontend workflow]].
